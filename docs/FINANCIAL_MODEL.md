## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

| Stream | Mechanism | % of Revenue (Steady State) |
|--------|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Subscription** | Monthly/annual plans (Pro, Team, Enterprise) | 40% |
| **Usage-based** | Compute hours, proxy requests, storage overage | 45% |
| **Enterprise contracts** | Annual contracts with custom pricing | 15% |

### Pricing Assumptions

| Tier | Monthly Price | ARPU (with usage) | Conversion Rate | Avg Lifetime |
|------|--------------|-------------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | — | — |
| Pro | $29/mo | $52/mo (including usage overage) | 8% of free users (60-day window) | 18 months |
| Team | $99/seat/mo | $340/mo (avg 3.4 seats) | 15% of Pro users (6-month window) | 28 months |
| Enterprise | Custom | $2,800/mo (avg contract) | Direct sales | 36 months |

### Customer Acquisition Model

| Channel | % of Signups | CAC | Conversion to Paid |
|---------|-------------|-----|-------------------|
| Organic (SEO, content) | 45% | $0 | 8% |
| Community (Reddit, Discord, Twitter) | 25% | $0 | 10% |
| Viral (referrals, deployra.run URLs) | 15% | $0 | 6% |
| Paid (ads, sponsorships) — starts Month 9 | 15% | $35 | 5% |
| **Blended** | **100%** | **~$5** | **~8%** |

| Cost Component | Per Agent/Month | Notes |

## Scenario Analysis

### Base Case (Above Projections)

**Assumptions:**
- 8% free-to-paid conversion
- 5% monthly churn (paid)
- $52 Pro ARPU, $340 Team ARPU
- Organic-first growth, paid starts Month 9

| Year | ARR | Customers | Team Size | Burn |
|------|-----|-----------|-----------|------|
| 1 | $865K | 693 | 3 | -$586K |
| 2 | $6.7M | 3,532 | 10 | +$1.6M |
| 3 | $27.6M | 11,630 | 25 | +$11.8M |

### Bull Case

**Assumptions:**
- 12% free-to-paid conversion (strong product-market fit)
- 3% monthly churn
- $65 Pro ARPU, $450 Team ARPU (higher usage)
- Viral coefficient 0.5 (strong word-of-mouth)
- Enterprise sales close faster

| Year | ARR | Customers | Team Size | Burn |
|------|-----|-----------|-----------|------|
| 1 | $1.4M | 1,100 | 3 | -$430K |
| 2 | $12M | 6,500 | 14 | +$4.8M |
| 3 | $50M | 22,000 | 35 | +$25M |

**Trigger:** HN/PH launch goes viral, one or more high-profile companies adopt publicly, LangChain/CrewAI recommend Deployra in their docs.

### Bear Case

**Assumptions:**
- 4% free-to-paid conversion (weak activation)
- 8% monthly churn (retention issues)
- $40 Pro ARPU, $250 Team ARPU
- Slower organic growth
- AWS or LangChain launches competing feature

| Year | ARR | Customers | Team Size | Burn |
|------|-----|-----------|-----------|------|
| 1 | $320K | 280 | 3 | -$720K |
| 2 | $1.8M | 1,200 | 6 | -$400K |
| 3 | $6M | 3,500 | 12 | +$1.2M |

**Mitigation:** If bear case materializes by Month 6, reduce burn by deferring Hire #3, cut marketing to zero, focus exclusively on activation and retention improvements. 18-month runway extends to 24+ months.
